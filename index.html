About Me


Hi there! I’m Valeria — a Colombian-American junior at the University of Central Florida (UCF), currently majoring in Marketing with a minor in Digital Media.

Originally, I began my journey in Digital Media because I was drawn to the creative potential of web design and coding. However, after exploring both fields, I realized that my true passion lies in understanding how creativity and strategy intersect to create impactful digital experiences. This led me to switch my major to Marketing, while continuing to nurture my skills in web development as a minor.

I have experience with HTML and CSS and am constantly expanding my toolkit, exploring new tools and frameworks. Outside of school, I love gaming, playing board games, and crafting. I’m eager to continue learning and growing, combining my marketing knowledge with my digital media skills to craft innovative, meaningful experiences that make a real impact.
