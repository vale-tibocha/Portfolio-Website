Spring 2025 Showcase Presentation
End of Semester Showcase Presentation by Website and Microsoft Teams Team Spring 2025
This presentation was created for the End of Year Showcase hosted by the Arboretum. Each LxL team had the opportunity to present their work from the semester at different stations, and three teams were selected to give a formal 10-minute presentation at the end of the night. Our team was one of the selected groups, and we showcased our newest app, discussed recent website updates, and explained the importance of quality-of-life updates.
LxL Team Signs
Learning x Leading Team Signs by Valeria Tibocha-Florez
Halfway through my internship I was asked to design signs representing each LxL team. These signs were used during our Women Supporting Science Event, our End of Year Showcase, and are intended for future use as well. I made sure to capture what each team does while maintaining some individuality in each. My biggest challenge was creating signs for some of the less outdoorsy teams like "Business Operations" and my team, "Website and Microsoft Teams." Included on the website team sign is an easter egg that my team found amusing. The Arboretum mentors were grateful for the sign designs and will be using them for all future LxL endeavors.
